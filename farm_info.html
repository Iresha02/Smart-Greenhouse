• Hand pollination may increase yield in closed systems

🌡️ Temperature Control Issues
▼

Common Problems:

  • Check heating/cooling system functionality and settings
  • Inspect ventilation systems for blockages or malfunctions
  • Verify temperature sensor accuracy and placement
  • Examine insulation integrity and seal gaps
  • Review automated control system programming
  • Consider external factors (weather, equipment heat)
💧 Irrigation Problems
▼

Troubleshooting Steps:

  • Check water source and pump functionality
  • Inspect drip lines and emitters for clogs
  • Test soil moisture sensors for accuracy
  • Verify timer and controller settings
  • Examine water pressure and flow rates
  • Clean filters and replace worn components
🐛 Pest Management
▼

Integrated Approach:

  • Identify pest species through careful observation
  • Assess pest population levels and damage
  • Remove affected plant material immediately
  • Introduce beneficial insects if appropriate
  • Apply targeted treatments (organic preferred)
  • Implement preventive measures for future
🦠 Disease Control
▼

Disease Management Protocol:

  • Identify disease through symptoms and lab testing
  • Isolate affected plants to prevent spread
  • Remove and dispose of infected material properly
  • Improve environmental conditions (humidity, airflow)
  • Apply appropriate fungicides or bactericides
  • Monitor recovery and implement prevention
💡 Lighting Issues
▼

Lighting Troubleshooting:

  • Check electrical connections and circuit breakers
  • Test individual LED panels or bulbs
  • Measure light intensity at plant level
  • Clean light fixtures and reflective surfaces
  • Verify timer and dimming controls
  • Replace failed components promptly
📊 Sensor Calibration
▼

Sensor Maintenance:

  • Compare sensor readings with calibrated instruments
  • Clean sensor probes and housing regularly
  • Check cable connections and power supply
  • Recalibrate using manufacturer's procedures
  • Replace sensors showing consistent drift
  • Document calibration dates and procedures
